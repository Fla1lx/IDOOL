# IDOOL Game Design

## Game Description

IDOOL is a mobile idle / tycoon game set in an auto repair shop. The player receives cars, repairs them over time, earns money, and reinvests that money into the workshop.

The first version focuses on a clear and readable management loop instead of a large amount of content.

## Player Role

The player is the owner and manager of a small auto repair shop. They decide when to start repairs, what upgrades to buy, and how to grow the workshop from a single repair bay into a more efficient business.

## Core Game Loop

1. A car arrives in the queue.
2. The player sends the car to the repair bay.
3. A repair timer starts.
4. The repair completes and the player earns money.
5. The player spends money on workshop upgrades.
6. The upgraded workshop repairs future cars faster or for better rewards.

## Base Mechanics

- Incoming car queue.
- One active repair bay for the MVP.
- Repair jobs with duration and reward.
- Money balance.
- Upgrade purchases.
- Persistent progress between sessions.
- Simple UI for queue, repair timer, money, and upgrades.

## MVP Scope

- One workshop screen.
- One repair bay.
- Several simple repair types.
- Money rewards for completed repairs.
- Basic upgrade path for speed, reward, and queue capacity.
- Local save system.
- Minimal UI and placeholder-friendly visuals.

## Out of Scope for Initial MVP

- Multiple locations.
- Staff hiring and worker automation.
- Complex car customization.
- Deep narrative or quest system.
- Online features, leaderboards, or cloud saves.
- Ads, in-app purchases, and live operations.
- Advanced 3D art pipeline or final production assets.
